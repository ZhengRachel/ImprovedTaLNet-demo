Demos for "Improved methods based on pseudo target generation and domain adversarial training for voice reconstruction from silent tongue and lip articulation. "

Paper: TO DO

Authors: Rui-Chen Zheng, Zhen-Hua Ling

Abstract: This paper mainly focuses on the task of voice reconstruction from ultrasound tongue images and optical lip videos in silent speaking mode, where people only activate their intra-oral and extra-oral articulators without producing sound. We propose to employ a method built on pseudo targets generation and domain adversarial training with a new iterative training strategy to improve the intelligibility and naturalness of speech recovered from silent tongues and lips. Experiments show that our proposed method dramatically improves the intelligibility of speech reconstructed in silent speaking mode compared to the baseline. When using an ASR model for speech recognition to measure intelligibility, the WER of our proposed method decreases by over 15%. At the same time, our proposed method also outperforms the baseline on the intelligibility of speech reconstructed in vocalized articulating mode, reducing WER by about 10%.
